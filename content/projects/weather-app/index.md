End-to-end digital strategy for an early childhood education platform, from UX-focused website design to Meta Ads optimization and community growth.

## Overview

Happy Brains is an early childhood education platform powered by teachers, nursing professionals, and pedagogy experts, supporting the development of children aged 1–8 and their families. I led the full digital transformation, designing and launching a modern website from scratch while managing Meta Ads, community engagement, content strategy, and ongoing platform optimization.

## Key Achivements

### Web Design & UX/UI

- Designed and developed a fully custom, high-conversion website using Figma and Framer, with strong emphasis on intuitive navigation and emotional design  
- Implemented mobile-first, responsive architecture tailored to parent behavior and device usage  
- Created a cohesive visual identity that communicates trust, warmth, and educational excellence


### Performance Marketing & Community
- Managed full-funnel Meta Ads campaigns (awareness + conversion) targeting parents of children 1–8  
- Built and nurtured an active parent community across social platforms, fostering trust and engagement  
- Developed integrated content strategy across blog, social media, and email nurturing flows


### Perform Management
- Full ownership of ongoing website maintenance, content updates, and continuous UX improvements  
- Established data-driven optimization processes using analytics and user feedback

## Challenges & Solutions

### Building from Zero
**Problem**: No existing digital presence or brand assets. Needed a complete website, visual identity, and marketing infrastructure launched quickly.  

**Solution**: Delivered a fully custom Framer website with complete UX/UI design, brand system, and technical foundation in record time.

### Reaching & Converting Parents

**Problem**: Broad target audience required balancing awareness and enrollment conversions effectively.  

**Solution**: Designed layered Meta Ads strategy with segmented audiences by child age and parent interests, paired with optimized landing pages and clear calls-to-action.

### Multi-Channel Consistency

**Problem**: Risk of fragmented messaging across website, ads, social, and email.  

**Solution**: Created a unified content calendar and parent journey framework that aligned all channels for a seamless experience.


## Results 

- Launched a modern, high-quality digital platform with excellent UX/UI and strong mobile performance  
- Meta Ads campaigns successfully driving both brand awareness and enrollment inquiries  
- Established an engaged parent community and consistent content engine
